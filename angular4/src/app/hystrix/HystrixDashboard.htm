Hystrix Dashboard

This Hystrix Dashboard displays the real time metrics of all sessions' circuits.


Since all of the metrics are piped into the dashboard, the two ciruits that you care about are the ones prefixed with your session id.


The msgFactory circuit is the Hystrix Command that wraps around the call to the Message Factory.

Calls to the message factory can be increased or decreased in the control panel.


The sendMsg circuit is the Hystrix Command that wraps the call that sends the message to the dashboard

Provided that the circuit breaker pattern works, the flow of requests to send a message should remain constant.

Therefore promoting a constant laminar flow of our message stream.


For even more information you can visit the Hystrix Wiki
